Specializations

How I Can Help

I specialize in helping individuals and couples address a range of challenges through compassionate, evidence-based therapy.

Relationship patterns and emotional closeness

If you find yourself stuck in repeating patterns—emotional distance, conflict, or fear of intimacy—I can help you understand the deeper dynamics at play. Using a relational, TA-based approach, we explore early experiences and scripts that shape how you connect and work toward building healthier, more secure relationships.

  • Identify recurring relationship patterns
  • Understand attachment styles and needs
  • Develop skills for authentic connection
  • Navigate conflict with greater ease
Discuss Your Needs

Burnout, pressure and role fatigue

If you're feeling stretched thin or constantly on edge, therapy offers a space to slow down and reconnect. Together, we explore the roles you've taken on—at work, in your family, or both—and gently shift the patterns that lead to emotional exhaustion, perfectionism, and loss of self.

  • Recognize early signs of burnout
  • Set healthier boundaries
  • Reconnect with your core values
  • Develop sustainable self-care practices
Discuss Your Needs

Neurodiversity and self-acceptance

Whether you're diagnosed or self-identifying as neurodivergent (ADHD, Autism, Highly Sensitive, or other), I provide a supportive, affirming space to process your experience. We explore how masking, emotional regulation, or being misunderstood may have shaped your self-image—and how to reclaim your strengths and identity.

  • Understand your unique neurological profile
  • Develop personalized coping strategies
  • Navigate social and professional environments
  • Build confidence in your authentic self
Discuss Your Needs

Cultural identity, migration and belonging

Moving between cultures can impact your sense of self and belonging. Drawing from my own experience and training, I help you navigate cultural transitions, identity questions, and finding your authentic voice across different contexts.

  • Process cultural transition experiences
  • Integrate different aspects of identity
  • Address feelings of displacement or isolation
  • Build a sense of belonging on your own terms
Discuss Your Needs

Low self-esteem and inner criticism

When your inner voice is critical, harsh, or never satisfied, it can impact every part of your life. I help you explore where that voice came from and how to transform it. Using a compassionate, trauma-informed approach, we work toward a more grounded and accepting sense of self.

  • Identify sources of self-criticism
  • Develop self-compassion practices
  • Challenge negative core beliefs
  • Build a healthier relationship with yourself
